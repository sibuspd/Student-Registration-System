Student Registration System

A comprehensive platform designed to facilitate the registration and management of student information. Students can easily register their details, including Name, allotted Id, Email, and Contact number. The system also provides robust functionalities for editing and deleting records, ensuring that the information remains accurate and up-to-date. This user-friendly system streamlines the administrative process, making it efficient and effective for both students and administrators.

Note. The Entry numbers will remain unchanged for reference.

Warning.The total count of all entries since the use of this app will remain intact across sessions and tabs regardless of deletion/updation of records. This is to find any specific student against its entry number.

Features

  • Persistent Data
  • Edit/Delete Records
  • Enhanced Readability
  • Valid Input Verification
  • Dynamic Scrollbar for Easy Navigation
